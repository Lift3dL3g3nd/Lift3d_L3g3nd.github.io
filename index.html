Bounty Hive

Bounty Hive focuses on the secure custody, approval, and controlled delivery of exploit-grade vulnerability proof-of-concept artifacts.

Many vulnerability disclosure workflows assume trust-based handling of PoCs. Once a working exploit exists, that assumption can introduce unnecessary security, legal, and audit risk.

What Bounty Hive Does

  • Maintains controlled custody of exploit artifacts
  • Enforces role-based access and staged disclosure
  • Provides auditability over who accessed what, and when

What Bounty Hive Is Not

  • Not a vulnerability scanning engine
  • Not a replacement for existing bug bounty platforms
  • Not a marketplace for exploit code

Who This Is For

  • Security teams operating bug bounty or VDP programs
  • Legal and compliance teams concerned with exploit handling
  • Organizations seeking safer post-discovery workflows
